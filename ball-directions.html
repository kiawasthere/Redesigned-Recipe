  • Grease a 9x13 inch baking dish. Set aside
  • Add vegetable oil to a 4 quart saucepan, and heat
  • when corn stops popping. remove from heat and put popcorn in new pan
  • Melt butter in a medium saucepan over low heat. Stir in marshmallows and cool until melted, stirring constantly.
  • Pour marshmallow mixture over popcorn and mix with spoon to coat evenly.
  • Let mixture cool slightly. Smear butter on your hands or spray with non-stick cooking spray.
  • Mix popcorn with your hands so that it is evenly coated
  • Form popcorn into 8 balls

    • Takes 30 min to prepare
