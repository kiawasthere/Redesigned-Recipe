small but flavorful!!

  • Preheat oven to 350 degree F, Butter six 4-inch and place in 9x13 baking pan
  • Lemon zest, and 2 tablespoons white sugar together in a small bowl
  • Mirowave 2 tablespoons butter in a microwave-safe bowl until melted about 30 secs
  • Stir in brown sugar, spread on ramekin
  • Beat 1/2 cup and butter and 1/2 cup white sugar in a large bowl in a mixture
  • Add eggs 1 at a time, mixing and mix in lemon juice and vanilla extract
  • Add flour, baking powder, ginger and salt and stir. Mix in milk
  • Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean
  • 25 to 35 minutes
  • Let cool and take out of pan

    • Takes 50 min to prepare
