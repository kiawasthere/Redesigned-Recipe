Perfect for the holidays!

  • Bake the cake, mix the eggs, pumpkin and cinnamon in a bowl
  • Add to egg mixture, blend the batter until smooth
  • Spread the batter in a prepared jelly roll pan and preheated oven until the cake springs back when touched
  • Roll the cake, in a towel and let it finish cooling
  • Make the filling, beat the cream cheese, sugar, butter and vanilla in a mixing bowl until smooth
  • When the cake is cool and unroll it and add the cream cheese
  • Roll the cake again without the towel and dust sugar

    • Takes 45 min to prepare
