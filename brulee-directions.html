Best Brulee, you tired yet!

  • Preheat oven to 325 degrees F
  • Heat the cream and vanilla in a saucepan over med heat
  • Whisk together the egg yolks, brown sugar, white sugar, cinnamom, salt, ginger. cloves and pumpkin in a bowl
  • Slowly pour 1 cup of a cream mixture into the egg mixture.
  • pour the egg mixture into the saucepan and whisk. and pour into ramekins
  • Bake in preheated oven until set about 15 mins and refrigerate for 4 to 6 hours
  • Before serving, sprinkle 1 teaspoon of sugar over the top of creme brulee
  • Use a kitchen torch or your oven's broiler to caramelize the sugar
  • it may take 2 to 3 minutes in broiler and serve

    • Takes 25 min to prepare
